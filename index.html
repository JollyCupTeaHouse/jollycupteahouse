About Us

Jolly Cup is a shop selling smoothies, tea, and Vietnamese food. The store has been established for nearly 20 years in the Spring Brand area. We serve our customers delicious dishes of Vietnamese cuisine. The shop's smoothies are made with fresh fruit; customers can mix and match their desired fruits. You can also enjoy our food and drinks at home through Uber Eats, Doordash and Grubhub delivery.
